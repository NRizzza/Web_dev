POEM

You are not what you look like
You're the music you listen to, the shows you watch, the art you make, the flowers in your hair, and your favorite blanket.
You're not the pimple on your nose or the pudge on your tummy.
You're not your thighs or your teeth. You are the color ofyour hair. You're the mismatched socks you wear and your favorite sweater.
You're not what you look like or the body you are in.
You are what you love.
