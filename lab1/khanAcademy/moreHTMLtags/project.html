Strawberry cake

  • Time:80 min
  • Serves:2-3
Ingredients for biscuit Quantity
flour 173 g
eggs 5
powered sugar 195 g
butter 45 g
milk 68 g
vanilla essence 1.5 teaspoons
baking powder 0.5 teaspoons
salt 0.25 teaspoon
Ingredients for Cream Quantity
Sludge 33-35% 700 g
sugar powder 56g
cheese mascarpone(or sweetener) 50g
honey 100g
strawberry 1-1.2kg

Biscuit:

Step 1: Heat the eggs, sugar and glucose in a water bath until it reaches about 45-50 C. Remove and beat until white. Reduce the speed of the mixer to a minimum and beat for another 7 minutes.

Step 2: In a separate bowl, heat the milk, butter and vanilla essence until all the butter has melted. Sift the flour, add baking powder, salt and gently mix with the egg mixture. Then add 1/4 of the dough to the butter-milk mixture and beat. Then add to the test.

Step 3: Pour the dough into a 30 × 40 mold and bake at 190-200 degrees Celsius for about 10 minutes or until golden brown. Transfer to a grate and let cool.

Cream:

Step 1: Mix all the ingredients in a chilled bowl and whisk on high speed until stiff peaks. You can not use a thickener, if there is none, by increasing the amount of cheese.

Finally

Step1:Cut the crusts from the biscuit, divide it into two parts. Soak the sponge cake with syrup, lay the strawberries cut in half, then cover with cream ¾ of the total mass, align, lay the second layer of the sponge cake. The second layer is also soaked with syrup, put the rest of the cream on the surface, smooth, decorate with strawberries. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

Enjoy your cake❤
